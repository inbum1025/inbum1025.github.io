Profile

I am a master's student at Chungnam National University working on document intelligence, OCR post-correction, document layout analysis, and multimodal model evaluation. My recent work includes layout error detection benchmarks, Korean OCR correction, visually-rich document understanding, and zero-data-leakage evaluation of LLMs on the 2026 Korean CSAT mathematics exam.
