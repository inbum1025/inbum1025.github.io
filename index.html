About Me

I am a master's student in Computer Science & Engineering at Chungnam National University, working with the iSoft Lab on document intelligence, optical character recognition, document layout analysis, and vision-language models.

My research focuses on building datasets, benchmarks, and evaluation pipelines for reliable document understanding systems. Recent work includes layout error detection, OCR post-correction, visually-rich document understanding, and zero-data-leakage evaluation of LLMs on the 2026 Korean CSAT mathematics exam.
